The system will have an ATC (Air Traffic Controller) named STC (Sector Traffic Controller)

The ATC will direct space ship in the system.

Work Flow of the STC

Init:
	- Instanciastes the System Object
        	- Creates and internal representation of system objects to check and issue collision advisories (body radius)
                - For each station and surface for
			- Check for station type and loads a station type manifesto that contains the approach final vector and the collision radius 
	- Receives in system ship manifestos
		- Ship manifesto includes (ShipId, Call Sign, Turn Capabilities, Acceleration and Deceleration capabilities, Position, IFR or VFR operation, destination and flight plan ir any)


Operation:

         - STC radar will operate in an 1Hz frequency
		- Receives ship update pings at this frequency (via a queue) (the update ping will have direction vectors, speed)
		- Calculates waypoints according with the flight plan, collisions and traffic


Flight plan:

	- A ship will ask STC for a flight plan to a specific destination
	- The STC will calculate an initial plight plan and assign it to the ship internal STC representation
	- The flight plan will be composed by Flight Segments (Departure; EnRoute; Approach; CloseFinal)
		- Departure: the STC will compute a departure path; 
			- For surface ports it will include a climb to 500meters, a coasting phase of >1Km, a turn away from the planet but in the general direction of the destination but away from nearby bodies
                        - For space station it will include a coasting phase of >1Km, a turn away from the planet and the station but in the general direction of the destination but away from nearby bodies
                        - For ships already in space it will include a turn in the general direction of the destination but away from nearby bodies

		- EnRoute: the STC will compute a route path to 500km of the destination including any turns to avoid system bodies and objects
			- the route will have an acceleration phase a coast phase an a deceleration phase: 
				- those phases will be aligned with the ship capabilities
			- Ate the end of this phase the ship should be at a speed of 10MM/s (million meters)
		- Approach
			- The STC will calculate an approach route that will take the ship to 10km away from the destination (taking into account obstacles such as stations and planet surfaces)
                        - At the end of this phase the ship will need to have its speed <500km/h
                - CloseFinal
			- The STC will calculate vectors to guide the ship to short final vector
	- Other phases
		- ApproachHold: if a ship will get to the Approach start point at more than 10MM/s and will need to be recomputed a ApproachHold to correct the speed
		- MissedApproach: if a ship will get to the CloseFinal at a speed greater than 500km/h a MissedApproach route will need to be calculated to correct

Route check:
       - The STC should calculate route deviations (expected vs actual)
       - The deviation function should check velocity and route
       - The allowed error for route should be a function that will take into account speed and ship capacilities
       - The allowed error for speed should have an allowed percentage of error

Phases:
    - None = 0,
    - Departure,
    - EnRoute,
    - Approach,
    - ApproachHold,
    - CloseFinal,
    - MissedApproach

Orders / Advisories the STC can issue:

    - Turn Left/Right Heading <1 360> (heading is system heading)
    - Pitch Up/Down Vertical Heading <1 360>  (heading is system heading)
    - Rotate Left/Right Angle <-180 +180> (used to align the ship with the system plain and help navigation)
    - Accelerate to XXX Km/h
    - Decelerate to XXX Km/h
    - Intercept and follow Station Approach beacon (when delivering ship to approach final vector)
