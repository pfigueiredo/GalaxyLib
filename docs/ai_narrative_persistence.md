# Specification: Narrative Persistence

## 1. The "Galaxy Log" (External DB)
Every major event involving an AI agent is written to a shared narrative table.
- **Example:** If a player causes a massive collision in the Sol system, the Sol SA writes a "Universal Alert" embedding.
- **Cross-System Impact:** When the player enters the Centauri system, the Centauri SA fetches recent "Player History" from the DB and may greet the player with: "We heard about the mess you made in Sol. Watch your vector here."

## 2. NPC Dynamic Quests
Quests are generated by NPCs using the ADK's access to the `GalaxyLib` state:
- **Trigger:** NPC looks for a `SystemBody` with specific `Resource` deficiency.
- **Goal:** "I need 50 tons of Silicates from Mars."
- **Persistence:** If the player fails, the NPC remembers. Next time they meet, the NPC's LLM prompt includes: "Still waiting on those Silicates, Vanguard."

## 3. Local Authority "Laws"
Laws are dynamic LLM instructions.
- **Emergency Law:** If the SA detects many ships with `collision_risk`, it may broadcast: "System is under Emergency Speed Limit. Max speed 200 km/h."
- **Enforcement:** The STC will now issue `DecelerateTo` orders to every ship. Disobeying triggers a Patrol intercept.
