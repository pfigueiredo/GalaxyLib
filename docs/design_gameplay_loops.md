# Design: LLM-Driven Emergent Loops

## 1. The "Political Intrigue" Loop
- **Trigger:** A System Authority (SA) is "Personality-Seeded" as *Paranoid*.
- **Action:** The SA hires the player to "Shadow" an NPC Merchant who has been behaving oddly.
- **LLM Interaction:** The player must stay within 5km of the Merchant (monitored by STC). The Merchant hails the player: "How much is the SA paying you? I'll double it if you delete the flight logs."
- **Outcome:** A permanent shift in System Law or Faction Power.

## 2. The "Procedural Lore" Loop
- **Action:** Exploration NPCs (Scouts) "discover" anomalies based on noise in the `Galaxy.bin` density map.
- **Narrative:** The LLM interprets a "density spike" as an "Ancient Ruin" or "Dead Station."
- **Quest:** The Scout sells the player the "Coordinates." The mission is chained to a "Historian NPC" who needs you to bring back artifacts.

## 3. The "Vengeance" Loop
- **Trigger:** Player destroys an NPC ship.
- **Persistence:** The NPC's "Relative" or "Organization" (another Agent) is flagged in the DB.
- **Outcome:** 10 hours later, in a completely different sector, an agent hails you: "My brother died in the Sol system. You have 20 seconds to prepare your shields."
