# Scenario: The Hostile Takeover
**Location:** Sol System (Mercury / Einstein Base)

### 1. The Conflict
The *Einstein extraction Corp* owns the base, but the *Sol Liberation Front* (SLF) has 45% influence and wants to seize the port.

### 2. The Player's Role
The player arrives with a load of industrial explosives. 
- **Corporate Hail:** "Delivery confirmed. Proceed to CloseFinal Pad 1."
- **Rebel Hail (Interrupt):** "Vanguard-01, don't land. Redirect to these coordinates in the shadow of Mercury. We have a better offer, and we'll clear your STC record."

### 3. The Climax
- **Choice A (Corporate):** Land normally. The SLF attacks. The SA scrambles patrols to protect you.
- **Choice B (Rebel):** Deviate from the path. The SA issues a `ROUTE DEVIATION` and marks you hostile. The Rebels provide a "Stealth STC Path" (a chain of waypoints through asteroid fields) where the SA radar can't see you.

### 4. Outcome
Completing Choice B shifts the system into **Lockdown** state, and the SLF becomes the Ruling Faction of Einstein Base.
