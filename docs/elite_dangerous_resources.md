# Reference: Elite Dangerous Resource List

This document contains a comprehensive list of natural resources (Metals, Minerals, and Raw Elements) from *Elite Dangerous*. This list serves as a reference for expanding the `GalaxyLib` resource system.

## 1. Mining Commodities (Metals & Minerals)
These are primarily found in asteroid rings (Metallic, Metal-Rich, Rocky, and Icy).

### High-Value / Core Mining
*   **Minerals:** Monazite, Musgravite, Alexandrite, Void Opals, Grandidierite, Serendibite, Benitoite, Low Temperature Diamonds.
*   **Metals:** Rhodplumsite.

### Common / Laser Mining
*   **Metals:** Platinum, Osmium, Palladium, Gold, Silver, Samarium, Praseodymium, Indite, Gallite, Bertrandite, Coltan.
*   **Minerals:** Painite, Bromellite, Tritium (Fuel), Lithium Hydroxide, Liquid Oxygen, Methane Clathrate.

---

## 2. Raw Materials (Elements)
These elements are used for Engineering and Synthesis. They are found on planetary surfaces (via SRV prospecting) or as byproducts of mining.

| Grade | Rarity | Materials |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **G1** | Very Common | Carbon, Iron, Nickel, Phosphorus, Sulphur, Lead, Rhenium |
| **G2** | Common | Chromium, Manganese, Vanadium, Zinc, Arsenic, Germanium, Selenium |
| **G3** | Standard | Cadmium, Mercury, Molybdenum, Niobium, Tin, Tungsten, Boron |
| **G4** | Rare | Antimony, Polonium, Ruthenium, Technetium, Tellurium, Yttrium |

---

## 3. Environmental Distribution (Reference)
*   **Metallic Rings:** High concentration of high-tier metals (Platinum, Gold).
*   **Metal-Rich Rings:** Broad variety of industrial and precious metals.
*   **Rocky Rings:** High-value core minerals (Monazite, Musgravite).
*   **Icy Rings:** Volatiles and high-value crystals (Void Opals, Low Temp Diamonds).
*   **Planetary Surfaces:** Raw elements determined by planet type and geological activity.
